Wallace Falls State Park

Trail information
Located in Gold Bar, this trail is 5.6 miles round trip with an elevation gain of 1300ft. You do not have to go to the top to see the falls though. There are lower and middle viewing areas that are just as beautiful if not more. The trail is mostly shady and popular on weekends, although to get to the trail, you have to walk on a gravel road for 1/4 of a mile with power lines overhead and no shade. The trail name is Woody Trail and it's off to the right along the river. Dogs are allowed. There is a bathroom at the trailhead.
Location
The address is: 14503 Wallace Lake Road Gold Bar, WA 98251
Parking
A Discover Pass is required. Parking is limited and parking on Ley Road is prohibited. Getting there early is important on the weekends.
