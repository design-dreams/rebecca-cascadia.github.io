Twin Falls

Trail information
Located in North Bend, this trail is 2.6 miles round trip with an elevation gain of 500ft, although it feels like more because there are many ups and downs. Dogs are allowed. There is a pit toilet at the trailhead. This trail is my favorite. The sound of the river is amazing here and the falls are so beautiful. There is a bridge over the falls. This is the turn around point. You can't get lost here. There is a short trail off to the right side with stairs at one point. It's just a another place to view the falls, but they are pretty far away from this point.
Directions
  1. Traveling East on I-90, take exit 34.
  2. Turn right onto 468th Avenue SE.
  3. Shortly after, turn left onto SE 159th Street.
  4. The parking lot is at the end of this road.
Parking
A Discover Pass is required. Parking is limited.
