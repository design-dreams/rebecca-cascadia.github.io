Little Si Trail

Trail information
Located in North Bend, this trail is 4.7 miles round trip with an elevation gain of 1300ft. It is mostly shady and popular on weekends. There are some medium sized boulders on the trail, so larger steps are required. Dogs are allowed. There is a bathroom at the trailhead.
Directions
  1. Traveling on I-90 east, take Exit 32.
  2. Turn left onto 436th Ave SE.
  3. Turn left onto SE North Bend Way.
  4. Turn right onto SE Mount Si Road.
  5. Just after crossing the river, look for the parking area on the left.
Parking
A Discover Pass is required. The 40 space lot can become full on weekends. Getting there early helps. There is an overflow lot nearby, but that can fill up also.
