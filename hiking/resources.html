External Resources

Washington Trails Association
A great resource for hikes in Washington along with current trip reports. This non-profit also organizes volunteers to maintain trails and advocates for protecting these important natural areas.
Discover Pass
Required in many places for parking near trails and other natural areas. It can be ordered online and sent by mail for $35. Just don't forget to hang it on your rear view mirror when you go.
Washington State Parks
Find a state park and information about it.
The Mindful Path to Self-Compassion by Christopher K. Germer, PhD
Wonderful book with many possibilities for meditation. Must read for anyone struggling with self worth.
Mindfulness by Ellen J. Langer
Very interesting book about what mindfulness is. References scientific studies.
